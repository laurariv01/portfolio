From Veterinary to Coding

My Trek to Tech

I am an animal lover with a secret passion for technology. I wanted to be a veterinarian (I thought), until every doctor I ever spoke to told me not to do it. So before spending money I didn't have on vet school, I became a veterinary nurse to see if that was the right move for me- it wasn't. I LOVE working with animals, but just knew I was meant for more. I held a few lead positions throughout my career as a veterinary nurse, and saw my talents lay in leadership. I also held online businesses on the side (because being a vet tech makes no money- I needed side hustles).

Running my side hustles showed me my TRUE passion lies in business. So I went back to graduate school to get my MBA. In graduate shcool, I took a course early on about information technology and how it is utilized in the world of business. I became fascinated with all aspects of technology in business-product management, programming, and data science. The best stepping stone from vet tech to business was FIGO pet insurance- the job that combined my animal wellness skills with my business savvy, and the job I recently left to dedicate to coding full-time!

Working for a start-up, I saw all the facets of business, my favorite one was IT. Helping test for our app, and insurance software intrigued me, being part of that opened my mind, and I fell in love with coding while working on a project with the COO who is my mentor. So here I am, ready and eager to learn."
